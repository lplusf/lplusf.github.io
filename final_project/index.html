This is Me

Early Life

I was born and raised in Greenwich, CT. I was raised in an Irish-Italian family with 2 older sisters. We have grown to be very close over the years, I do consider them my best friends.

Teenage Years

At the age of 15, I lost my mother to cancer. This was a very difficult time in my life, and I started to go into a downward spiral. There were a few things that I wish I could do over:

  • Focused more on School
  • Cared more about myself
  • Spent more time with my family

Present Day

I am at a really good place in my life now. I am working on my Masters degree and I have an awesome job. My passion is reading and writing and I hope to one day become a famous author.
